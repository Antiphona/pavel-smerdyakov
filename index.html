A short text here explaining why I consider him to be the most important Karamazov brother, and the catalyst for their becoming.

Though he does not have the Karamazov name it is he who determines the trajectories of the remaining brothers, who replace him upon his death: Dmitri among the prisoners, condemned for the murder committed by Smerdyakov; Ivan in the madhouse; Alyosha the Revolutionary.
